Whiplash450's Dark Heresy 2nd Edition Character Creator 

What is it?

The idea of this site is to help new and veteran acolytes alike create a character for use in the now discontinued* Dark Heresy 2nd Editon RPG game. The game is set in the Warhammer 40k future universe by Games Workshop.
Up until Sept 2016 the Dark Heresy game was published by Fantasy Flight Games.

* GW and FFG ended their 8yr partnership and parted ways around the middle of Sept. 2016, meaning that all the FFG rpg's like Dark Heresy, Only War etc.. are now dead and out of print. More info about the split here
**Disclaimer: This site is in no way affiliated with either Games Workshop or Fantasy Flight Games.

How do I use it?

The generater flows through the steps of character generation, in the same order as the core rule book, and you just fill in the details and make choices regarding the origins and skill-set of your character.

Ok, I have a character what now?

The site provides the possibility to generate a .pdf of your character by writing the details onto the freely availble DH character sheet. You can then either save or print this for use in your games. (I am working on setting up a database to save generated characters to, so that you can pick up where you left off if you can't finish the process in one session)
